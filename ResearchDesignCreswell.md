## Research Approach
Three components
### Worldview
In proposals, declare worldview as position you take
* post-positivism
Cause and effect, quantitative focus
* constructivism
Social construct, looking at societal items as fabricated objects
* Transformative/critical
Targets roots of oppression
* Pragmatism
What practically works, practical solutions
### Research Designs
* Quantitative
Surveys, experimental
* Qualitative
Narrative, phenomenological, grounded, ethnography, case studies
* Mixed
Qualitative then quantitative (exploratory sequential), quantitative then qualitative (explanatory sequential), simultaneous (convergent mixed)
